# Philadelphia Salon Analysis — What Drives Salon Success in Philadelphia?

## Overview
This project analyzes what drives salon success in Philadelphia using the Yelp Open Dataset 
supplemented by original market research with Google businesses. As a licensed cosmetologist and data analyst, 
I developed a custom market focus taxonomy to uncover patterns in ratings, pricing, 
and geographic distribution across 12 top-rated Philadelphia salons.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas) — Google Colab
- Tableau Public
- Google Sheets

## Data Sources
- Yelp Open Dataset (150,000+ businesses filtered to Philadelphia salons)
- Original research (Google Maps, StyleSeat)

## Key Findings
- Luxury & Fashion salons generated the highest review volume (avg 701 reviews)
- Textured & Natural salons commanded the highest entry-level pricing ($48 avg minimum)
- Center City and Fairmount neighborhoods produced the highest rated salon clusters
- Niche branding consistently outperformed generalist salons in ratings and engagement
